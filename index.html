Data Visualization & Storytelling Challenge for Educators

Program Overview
"Information is the oil of the 21st century, and analytics is the combustion engine"

SmartBridge, in collaboration with Tableau has launched a data visualization and storytelling challenge to teach the Australian educators in data analytics using Tableau.

The program contains 15 Hrs. of online instructor-led training on data visualization and storytelling followed by a 10 day challenge to develop visualization dashboards and stories utilizing the given data resources.

All the Australian educators teaching in higher education institutions, irrespective of their field of specialization are eligible to apply for the this program.

    How to Participate?

    01
    Must be current Student/Educator

    You must be current Student or Educator associated with an accredited acedemic institution at the time of submission.

    02
    Download and Activate Tableau Desktop

    As a Student/Educator, you're elgible for a free Tableau Desktop / Tableau Prep through Tableau for Students/Tableau for Teaching.

    03
    Access Tableau eLearning

    Tableau eLearning is a web-based training that you can consume at you own pace. Complete the learning paths to build and advance your skills with Tableau Desktop.

    04
    Attend 5 Days Bootcamp

    Attend 5 Days live instructor-led hands-on training on Tableau desktop to understand the featuers and work with a real use-case to create vizzes.

    05
    Enroll for a challenge

    Choose any domine of your interest and enroll for the challenge. Download the dataset and create data viz ad story.

    Explore Challenges
    06
    Submit through Tableau Public

    You must publish your visualization, dashboard and story to your Tableau Public profile. Make sure that the data you're useing can be shared publicly.
